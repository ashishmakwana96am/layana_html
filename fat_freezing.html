How Fat Freezing Work ?

Fat Freezing targets specifically the fat cells by lowering the temperature and pinpoints the content of the fat cells causing them to crystalize. No other structures are affected by this process hte remaining content of the fat cell is then disposed of naturally through the body’s lymphatic system.

What Result To Expect From Fat Freezing

On average afer a fat Freezing treatment you will see a 20% – 40% reduction of the fat in the localised area. As the body takes several months to flush out the dead fat cells, results will be visible after 4 – 12 week.

What Will Experience During The Fat Reduction Treatment

Before the fat freezing procedure is started a anti-freeze pad is placed onto the area that will be treated to protect the skin. During the treatment the fat is drawn into a suction cup before freezing, this will povide a pulling sensation and the area will be cold, within a few minutes the area should go numb. The handpiece will be on the area for 30 – 60 minutes depending on the size of the area being treated.
