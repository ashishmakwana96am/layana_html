How Does It Work ?

3D Vjuve is an advanced Fractional CO2 Laser offering Visual Rejuvenation facial and body treatments. The system can be used to treat a wide variety of aesthetic concerns including: fine lines and wrinkles, dyschromia, pigmented lesions, skin surface irregularities and scars.

3D Visual Rejuvenation improves the thickness and hydration of the dermis and epidermis, resulting in a healthier, glowing, youthful appearance to the skin.

This device is not only used for facial treatments, CO2 technology is well proven in the treatment of stretch marks and loose skin.

Pre and Post Treatment

Pre treatment

Do not use products containing retinol or strong AHA fruit acids on the treatment area prior to or during your skin rejuvenation treatment

Do not sunbathe or use sunbeds 4 weeks prior to and after treatment

Post treatment

Sanitised cooling packs can be applied to the skin post treatment.

No hot baths or showers - tepid only. Skin should be kept dry for 3 days or until the skin normalises.

Cooled boiled water can be used to wash the skin for the first 3 days post treatment if necessary.

Anti inflammatory medication can be used if necessary.

No heat treatments i.e. saunas and steam rooms.

No deodorant, perfumes or scented body lotions/creams on treatment area.

No swimming or Jacuzzis.

SPF 30+ must be worn on exposed skin daily.

Flaking may occur over the following days, do not pick and scratch the skin as this may result in infection or scarring.

Do not use any exfoliation treatments/products on the treatment area for 4 weeks post treatment.

The 3D-Youth Serum product can be used on the skin to help with the healing process.

3D Vjuve is a safe and advanced treatment offering a minimally invasive procedure that embraces Co2 technology.

Treatments last anywhere between 15 and 45 minutes depending on which treatment you have.

There is slight tingling sensation and warmth during the treatment, however, a numbing cream is applied to the skin prior to treatment to reduce the sensation on the skin.

During treatment the skin will be stimulated and will become slightly pink. A similar feeling to when you have been in the sun! The pinkness can last for up to 48 hours, depending on your skin sensitivity.

Results are noticeable from approximately two weeks following your treatment, after this your skin will become smoother, tighter, lines and wrinkles reduced and pores tightened. The treatment is progressive, so a course is recommended for best results.

A course of three to five sessions are generally recommended, which would consist of one treatment per month.

There is no downtime with this treatment, however, your skin surface can become dry and slightly flaky as the new skin cells are stimulated in the deeper layers of the skin.
