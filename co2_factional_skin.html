Pre and Post Treatment

Pre treatment

  • Stop using ‘active’ skincare (3 days prior to treatment).

  • Avoid direct sun exposure.

  • Avoid using steam and sauna on the day of treatment.

  • Avoid using steam and sauna on the day of treatment.

  • Excessive facial hair will need to be trimmed prior to treatment.

  • Avoid use of aftershaves and perfumes on treatment area.

Post treatment

  • STAY HYDRATED

  • Your skin will look its best if you drink enough water throughout the day. Keep your body hydrated by consuming at least eight glasses a day.

  • DON'T FORGET YOUR SUNSCREEN

  • Protect your skin from UV rays. SPF 30+ must be worn on exposed skin daily.

  • If you can, avoid direct sunlight as much as possible.

  • USE QUALITY ANTI-AGING SKINCARE PRODUCTS

  • Use quality skincare products that contain anti-aging ingredients. Ingredients like hyaluronic acid, peptides, and vitamin C can help boost collagen and elastin production, which further reduces the appearance of fine lines.
