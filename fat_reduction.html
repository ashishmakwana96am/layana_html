Ultrasound Pre-care Instructions

    Avoid Aspirin/ibuprofen or any vitamins that my thin the blood 72hr before treatment

    Avoid eating 2 hrs. prior and 2 hrs. after treatment (4 Hours of no eating)

    Must eat after 4 hrs. or body will go into starvation mode and reject treatment.

    Drink plenty of water to facilitate the lymphatic system (Drink 2 litres of water dailybefore & after treatment. )

    Avoid caffeine 2 hours before and after treatment

    Eat a well balanced diet on the day of treatment, Refrain from all breadsrice, pastas, sodas, cookies, candies and any other unhealthy foods during the duration of receiving treatments.

Ultrasound Cavitation Post-Care Instructions :

Drink at least 1-2 liters of water immediately following the treatment.

Overall maintaining a low calorie, low carbohydrate, low fat, and low sugar diet for 24 hours pre-treatment and three days post-treatment will help achieve the best results.

Eat a well balanced diet as much as you can. Many find this to be a great starting point for maintaining a healthier lifestyle in the future.
