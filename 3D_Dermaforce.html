About 3D Dermaforce

Microneedling with Radio Frequency

Microneedling /RF is the solution for the ultimate ‘non-surgical face lift’ and is in extremely high demand after the extensive press coverage that the treatment has received throughout the press, with celebrities raving about the treatment. How Does It Work ?

3D Dermaforce advanced Microneedling/RF is an advanced method of skin rejuvenation that stimulates the body’s natural healing process to encourage collagen production of the dermis resulting in anti-ageing and skin tightening, however it can also be used to treat concerns such as hooded eyelids, and to improve skin laxity above the knees.

During treatment, the radio frequency energy is delivered through the needles. By using the non-insulated needles on 3D Dermaforce, it allows you to target both the deep dermis and epidermis layers of the skin as the energy is emitted throughout the entire surface area.

The radio-frequency wave penetrates the basal melanocyte barrier, heating the collagen fibres in the dermis to 55°C-65°C. This stimulates the body’s natural healing process, and as cell turnover increases as the body heals, this results in the production of collagen and elastin in these areas.

  • No exercise or strenuous activity that will cause sweating.

Frequently Asked Questions

3D Dermaforce Microneedling/RF is an advanced method of skin rejuvenation that stimulates the body’s natural healing process. This encourages collagen production in the dermis resulting in rejuvenated and tightened skin.

It is safe for use on the face and body. It will have a positive effect on loose skin, fine lines and wrinkles, sun damaged/ pigmented skin, stretch marks and pitted skin.

Your skin will look plump due to the increase in thickness of the dermal tissue caused by collagen production. This will give an improvement to lines, wrinkles and crepey skin. Pigmentation patches will improve and even the tone of the skin. Improves the quality of textured skin. Stretch marks and loose skin can also be improved during a course of treatments.

During treatment, the radio frequency energy is delivered through the gold plated, insulated needles allowing precise delivery to the dermis. There is a slight warmth and pressure in the skin during application. Your skin will be slightly pink after treatment and may feel similar to when you have been in the sun.

This treatment can be suitable for women and men over the age of 18. A full consultation will be given prior to treatment*

You will possibly experience pinkness in the skin and a slight swelling, this may last for up to three days. You skin may feel slightly dry, your therapists will recommend 3D hydrating products and 3D SPF for you to use at home daily.
